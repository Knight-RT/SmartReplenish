### Matrix 
The matrix is divided into two primary sections: Product Specifications (PS) and Software Requirement Specifications (SRS) to establishes bidirectional traceability.

#### Columns
- Serial: sequential identification

Project Specification (PS) Columns
- Category: Categories that either fall under Biz Ops or Tech 
- ID: as per PS codification
- Description: paraphrased PS requirements
- Stakeholder: role, appointment or job title
- Precedence: Essential, desirable, or acceptable

Software Requirements Specification Columns
- Category: Categories under chosen template
- ID: as per SRS codification
- Description: paraphrased SRS requirements
- Owner: role, appointment or job title
- Ranking: MosCow Prioritisation (must-have, should-have, could-have, won't-have)
